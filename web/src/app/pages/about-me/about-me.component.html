About Me

I am a software developer with over a years experience currently living in columbus Ohio. I mostly work with Angular, SQL, AWS Lambda in server-less full stack applications. I also do quite a bit of work with Xamarin/.NET for Android and IOS applications. I have also dabbled with Python, Ruby, MongoDB, Dynamo DB, Tensor Flow, and computer vision.

My favorite part about writing code is the taking complex and abstracting one piece at a time until it is simple. Unraveling something that is difficult to understand and leaving behind something intuitive. It is both satisfying and keeps a project maintainable and changeable in the future.

The majority of my experience is working remotely with small teams. I excel at the ability to communicate ideas and work as a member of a greater whole. The most important part of this is having confidence in your own ability, while staying humble and respecting both the ability and perspective of others. I also enjoy mentoring others and setting them up for success.

Besides developing I also love building computers, gaming, fitness, hiking and travel. I have done quite a bit of travel here in the USA but plan to see much more. If you would like to swap stories and chat for any other reason you can Contact Me here.
